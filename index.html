About Lincom

LINCOM is a community platform where students can learn about linux and software building and participate in the open source projects. The community arranges weekly workshops focusing on various activities of linux and open source. This community is for inculcating an open source culture in the institute. Linux is a family of open-source Unix-like operating systems based on the Linux kernel, an operating system kernel first released on September 17, 1991, by Linus Torvalds.
